# Outline

## LANs and Routers
 - Routers sit between end devices and the Internet
 - Routers process all traffic going to and from devices
 - Traffic within a network is not filtered by routers
 - What happens when two devices are on separate networks?

## Network Segmentation
 - Allows us to put a router in between multiple devices
 - Routers can log traffic, restrict connections, ...
 - Switch VLANs let us virtually separate devices
