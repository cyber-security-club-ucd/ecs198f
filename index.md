## Welcome
Welcome to the Spring Quarter 2026 offering of ECS 198F: Introduction to Applied Cybersecurity!
This course is designed as supplement instruction in cybersecurity theory (ECS 153A) through teaching 
technical cybersecurity skills and conducting hands-on cybersecurity labs.
Our goal is to help you gain practical experience in security and prepare you for cybersecurity
competitions, internships, and careers. 

## Course details
[Canvas](https://canvas.ucdavis.edu/courses/1089494) will be primarily used for assignment submission. 
Lecture notes will be posted [here](/notes) and students will be using the 
[Cyber Security Club at UC Davis](https://daviscybersec.org)' own 
[server infrastructure](https://auth.daviscybersec.org) for completing the assignments.
We also have a [discord channel](https://discord.com/channels/388814420773437441/1478915036759064666) on the club server for this class.

### Grading
Assignments comprise 100% of your grade. No quizzes or exams. All assignments are due at 23:59
Pacific Time on the due date and should be formatted as a report following the
[rubric](/assets/assignments/rubric.pdf).

### Late work policy
Up to 72 hours of late work accepted. Your grade will be reduced by 10% of the total grade per 24 hours (rounded up) of late submission. For example, if your original score was 90% and you submitted two days late, your final score will be 90% - (2 * 10%) = 70%. No submissions accepted after 72 hours.

### Academic integrity
All students are expected to adhere to the UC Davis Code of Academic Conduct.
No submission material should contain AI generated content. Violations may be reported to SJA.
